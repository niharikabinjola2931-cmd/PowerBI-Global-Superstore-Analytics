# 📊 Global Superstore Sales & Profit Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the Global Superstore dataset using **Power BI** to uncover insights related to sales, profitability, customer segments, product categories, shipping performance, and regional trends.

The objective of this dashboard is to help business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify high-performing areas, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify the most profitable states and regions.
* Understand customer segment contribution.
* Analyze product category and sub-category performance.
* Evaluate shipping preferences and order distribution.

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* Interactive slicers (State, City, Segment)
* Sales by Customer Segment
* Sales by Sub-Category
* Quantity Sold by Category
* Region-wise Profit Analysis
* Top 10 States by Profit
* Shipping Mode Analysis
* Business Insights Panel

---

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* California generated the highest profit (42.31K).
* Consumer customers contributed over 51% of total sales.
* Chairs were the highest-selling sub-category (705.69K in sales).
* The West region generated the highest overall profit.
* Standard Class was the most preferred shipping mode.

---

## 🚀 Business Recommendations

* Investigate the factors contributing to lower profitability in underperforming states.
* Compare discount strategies across regions to improve profit margins.
* Analyze product mix in low-performing regions.
* Continue strengthening high-performing customer segments while improving engagement with lower-contributing segments.
* Optimize shipping strategies to improve operational efficiency.
